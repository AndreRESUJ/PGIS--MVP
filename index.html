Participant Information and Consent

Purpose of study: This research investigates how inner-city hydroponic farming in abandoned buildings can strengthen food security in Tshwane. We ask councillors, NGOs, retailers, and residents to help identify abandoned/vacant buildings.

Voluntary participation: You may withdraw at any time without consequence. Only data you enter voluntarily will be recorded.

Confidentiality: You may provide your name or remain anonymous. Any identifying information will be anonymised in reporting.

Data handling: Points you add (with notes/status) will be stored locally until the researcher collects the export. You will not be able to export directly.

Risks/benefits: Minimal risks are anticipated. Your contributions help inform policy and planning for food security and urban renewal.
